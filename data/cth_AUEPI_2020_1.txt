This exemption is subject to the following conditions:

identified in clause 1 of this Schedule.
(2) Kirby HVAC&R Pty Ltd must:
(a) notify the GEMS Regulator when delivery of the air conditioners
identified in clause 1 is received;
(b) report sales of the units to the GEMS Regulator every 3 months from the
date of delivery; and
(c) report the amount of stock remaining at the end of the exemption period
(if any) to the GEMS Regulator.
