MJ Kelly Appellant
Forest Practices Authority Respondent
This is a decision setting the conditions upon which Forest Practices
Plan MAC 1434, as determined by the Tribunal in its decision
Number D6-2009
dated 11 August 2009, is certified.
its decision number D6-2009 dated 11 August 2009, the Tribunal ruled by
paragraphs 22 and 23 as follows:
decision of the Tribunal is that the Forest Practices Plan MAC1434 is amended by
reserving from forestry operations the area of
approximately 20 hectares
lying in the area to the north of the streamside reserve marked with the numeral
plan on folio 8/19 in the Tribunal’s file; and
certifying the plan so amended.
certified plan is to be upon conditions to be set by the Tribunal. If agreement
is reached between the Appellant and the Forest

to such conditions, and supplied to the Tribunal, the Tribunal will set those
conditions. In default
of agreed conditions being provided to the Tribunal
within 21 days of the date of this decision, the Tribunal will set such
as it determines appropriate, based upon submissions to be made in
writing by the parties within that period of 21 days.
“A” to this decision sets out the terms of the certified Forest
Practices Plan and conditions to be set by the
