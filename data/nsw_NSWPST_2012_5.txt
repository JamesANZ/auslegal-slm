HEALTH PRACTITIONER REGULATION
NATIONAL LAW (NSW) No 86a
PSYCHOLOGY TRIBUNAL OF
INQUIRY UNDER SECTION 167
STEPHEN JAMES WHYTE
MATTER NUMBER: PS 02/2011
REGISTRATION NUMBER: PSY
CITATION: HCCC v WHYTE (No 3)
[2012] NSWPST 5
DATE OF ORDERS: 16 October 2012
FINAL ORDERS
Having made a finding of professional misconduct (see
HCCC v Whyte (No 1)
and having conducted ‘phase two’ of the Inquiry
(a full Reasons for Decision to be published by the Tribunal in due course)
Tribunal Orders that (Stephen Whyte being referred to as the Practitioner
Practitioner’s Registration is cancelled from 5pm on 23 November 2012.
is a condition on the Practitioner’s Registration from 16 October 2012 to
5pm on 23 November 2012 (being the time on which
the Respondent’s
registration will be cancelled) that the Respondent is only to see each of his
current clients/patients on
a maximum of two occasions for the purpose of
terminating the therapeutic relationship, reporting to the referring
practitioner and
referring the clients/patients on as appropriate.
Practitioner is prohibited from providing the following health service as
Health Care Complaints Act 1993
unless and until
he is registered as a psychologist:
(ii) counselling
(iii) psychotherapy
(iv) hypnotherapy
(v) welfare services (as defined in
Health Care Complaints
(vi) any other mental health service and/ or
(vii) any other alternative/healing health care/therapies.
Practitioner is not entitled to apply for review of the Order cancelling his
registration or the Prohibition Order until 18 months
after the date of the
HCCC v Whyte (No 3)
Practitioner is to pay the costs of the HCCC as agreed or assessed by the
Tribunal. If agreement cannot be reached the parties
have leave to relist the
matter for the Tribunal to make an assessment of the
Publication
A copy of these Orders is to be forwarded by the Registry to:
1) The Respondent and his legal advisor,
2) The HCCC,
3) The Psychology Council of New South Wales,
4) The Psychology Board of Australia,
5) as the Council and Board see fit.
Dated: 16 October 2012
