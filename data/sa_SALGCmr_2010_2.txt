I am satisfied that the applicant has the necessary technical expertise for
the servicing of gaming machines.
Turning to the applicant’s ability to offer its services to all gaming
machine licensees in South Australia, I am not yet satisfied
applicant’s ability to satisfy this requirement either solely or by way of
affiliation with other holders of a gaming
machine service licence. I place a
condition on the grant of this licence such that
AMTEK SERVICES PTY LTD
must not commence operating as authorised by the licence until I am
satisfied that it meets this requirement.
I note that Amtek’s proposed local premises at 32 Holland Street,
Thebarton is being made ready for the purposes of facilitating
machine service operation. I place a condition on the grant of this licence
AMTEK SERVICES PTY LTD
must not commence operating as
authorised by the licence until a final inspection of the premises has been
conducted by my Office
and I am satisfied that the premises is ready to
facilitate the conduct of the business authorised by the licence.
