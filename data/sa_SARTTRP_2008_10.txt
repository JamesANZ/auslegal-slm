STURT RIVER CARAVAN PARK v CARLSON
(RP08/0035)
APPLICATION LODGED 5 September 2008
ORDERS SOUGHT Vacant possession, rent arrears
and security bond repayment.
RESIDENTIAL PARK TENANCY AGREEMENT DETAILS
Rented property: PS49/Van 34
Park Owner: Bevan
Resident: Gregory Carlson
Agreement commenced: 26 July 2008
Period of agreement: 90 days
Rent: $155.00per week
Bond: No record of Bond
Sworn evidence was given in person by Bevan Hefford as Park Owner.
The resident and anyone who lives at the rented property were given notice of
the hearing. The resident was not at the hearing.
Mr Hefford gave evidence that the tenant has now vacated the rented property
and the owner of the rented property has taken possession
of the rented
I will dismiss the application as the Park Owner has now possession. I will
give no order as related to the security bond as Mr Hefford
was not able to give
evidence relating to the correct security bond number.
Application dismissed.
