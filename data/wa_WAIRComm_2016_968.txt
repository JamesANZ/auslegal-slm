Barry Landwehr -v- Director General, Department of Education [2016] WAIRComm 968 (28 December 2016)
WESTERN AUSTRALIAN INDUSTRIAL RELATIONS COMMISSION
DIRECTOR GENERAL, DEPARTMENT OF
CHIEF COMMISSIONER P E SCOTT
WEDNESDAY, 28 DECEMBER 2016
CITATION NO.
2016 WAIRC 00968
WHEREAS this is an application pursuant to s 29(1)(b)(i) of the
Industrial Relations Act 1979
WHEREAS on 21 October 2016, the Commission issued Directions to program
the matter through to hearing ([2016] WAIRC 00840;
96 WAIG 1564)
WHEREAS on 29 November 2016, the Commission amended those Directions to
extend time to file witness statements ([2016] WAIRC 00898);
WHEREAS on 22 December 2016, the parties requested that the Directions
be amended to extend time for filing outlines of submissions;
WHEREAS the Commission is of the opinion that the proposed amendment is
expedient for the expeditious and just hearing and determination
of the matter.
NOWTHEREFORE, the Commission, pursuant to the powers conferred on it under
Industrial Relations Act 1979
, and by consent, hereby directs:
THAT Direction 6 and Direction 7 of the Directions that issued on
21 October 2016 ([2016] WAIRC 00840;
(2016) 96 WAIG 1564)
deleted and replaced by the following Directions:
“6. THAT the applicant is to file and serve an outline of submissions
and any list of authorities upon which he intends to rely
by no later than
Friday, 20 January 2017.
the respondent is to file and serve an outline of submissions and any list of
authorities upon which she intends to rely by no
later than Friday,
27 January 2017.”
CHIEF COMMISSIONER P E SCOTT
