APPEAL AGAINST DECISION TO TAKE
REMOVAL ACTION ON 23 MARCH 2016
WESTERN AUSTRALIAN INDUSTRIAL RELATIONS COMMISSION
MR JAMES MCMAHON DEPARTMENT OF
CORRECTIVE SERVICES
CHIEF COMMISSIONER P E SCOTT
SENIOR COMMISSIONER S J KENNER
COMMISSIONER T EMMANUEL
WEDNESDAY, 28 DECEMBER 2016
APPL 17 OF 2016
CITATION NO.
2016 WAIRC 00971
WHEREAS this is an appeal pursuant to s 106(2) of the
Prisons Act
against a decision to take removal action; and
WHEREAS on 28 September 2016, the WAIRC issued an Order ([2016] WAIRC 00780)
to stay this appeal until the appellant’s application
for judicial review
in the Supreme Court of Western Australia was determined; and
WHEREAS on 22 December 2016, the appellant filed a
Form 14 – Notice
of withdrawal or discontinuance
in respect of this appeal.
NOW THEREFORE, the WAIRC, pursuant to the powers conferred on it by the
Prisons Act 1981
Industrial Relations Act 1979
THAT this appeal be, and is hereby dismissed.
Chief Commissioner P E
For and on behalf of the
Western Australian Industrial Relations Commissiont
