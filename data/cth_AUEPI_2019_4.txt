for GEMS Regulator Services) Instrument 2019
I, Sean Sullivan, GEMS Regulator, acting in accordance with my powers under

Sean Sullivan
GEMS Regulator
Dated: 29 March 2019
Preliminary
Name of Instrument
This instrument is
Greenhouse and Energy Minimum Standards (Fees for GEMS Regulator
Services) Instrument 2019.
Commencement
This instrument commences
on the day after it is registered.
This instrument revokes the
Greenhouse and Energy Minimum Standards
(Variation of a Model’s Registration – Application Fees) Instrument
This instrument specifies the fees payable for identified
services provided by the GEMS Regulator associated with registrations under
Greenhouse and Energy Minimum Standards Act 2012
Subsection 77(1) of the GEMS Act provides that the GEMS
Regulator may charge fees for services provided by, or on behalf of, the GEMS
Regulator in the performance of his or her functions.
Interpretation
Unless the contrary
intention appears words and expressions used in this instrument have the same
meaning as given in the Act.
Application
This instrument applies to
the services identified in Schedule 1.
Column 2 of Schedule 1 specifies the fee
payable for the service provided by the GEMS Regulator identified in Column 1 of
Schedule 1: Fees payable
(section 8)
GEMS Regulator service
Application to vary a registration to cover additional models
(Act section 46)
Assessment of a voluntarily submitted additional test report for a
registered product, where a revised test method has been specified
replacement determination (Act sections 36 and 40)
