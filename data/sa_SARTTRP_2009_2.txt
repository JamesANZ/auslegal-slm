Hearing 4 February 2009 at Adelaide
EDWARDS (TRADING AS VIRGINIA GARDENS RESIDENTIAL PARK) v
RANKIN (RP08/0040)
This is the determination of an application lodged on 16 January 2008 by
Stephen Edwards on behalf of the owner of Virginia Gardens
Residential Park. It
seeks an order that part of a security bond be paid to the park owner.
Mr Ian Edwards, the park owner appeared and gave sworn evidence.
There was no appearance by, or on behalf of, the former resident Mr Gavin
On the basis of the evidence, I find that on 16 October 2008 a periodic
residential park agreement was entered into between Mr Ian
Edwards as park owner
and Mr Gavin Rankin as resident in relation to premises known as site 35
Virginia Gardens Residential Park.
Rent payable under the agreement was $82.00
per week payable weekly. A security bond of $164.00 was paid by Mr Rankin and
with the Commissioner for Consumer Affairs (bond no. 7000583).
I find that this agreement is subject to the
Residential Parks Act 2007
(“the Act”).
I find that on 25 November 2008 Mr Edwards caused a “Notice of
Exclusion” (Form E) to be served upon Mr Rankin. As a
result of the
service of this Notice, I am satisfied that Mr Rankin be vacated to the
residential park. I am satisfied that caused
the residential park agreement to
I find from Mr Edward’s rent record that rent was paid to and including
18 November 2008. It is due to and including 25 November
2008. I therefore
find that the sum of $82.00 is due to the park owner.
Section 116(1)(j)
of the Act, I order that the security bond of $164.00 held
by the Commissioner (7000583-7) be paid to the park owner in the sum of
and to the resident in the sum of $82.00.
