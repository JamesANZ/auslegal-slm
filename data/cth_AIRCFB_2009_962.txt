MELBOURNE, 30 DECEMBER 2009
This decision concerns an application by the Seven Network (Operations) Limited and its related entities, Network Ten Pty Ltd and
its subsidiaries, Nine Network Pty Ltd and its related entities, WIN Corporation Pty Ltd and its related entities, Prime Media Group
and its related entities, TX Australia and Macquarie Southern Cross Media to vary the
Broadcasting and Recorded Entertainment Award 2010
(the modern award) to correct a number of typographical errors and clarify some entitlements for journalists covered by the award.
The application is made on a consent basis with the Media Entertainment and Arts Alliance (MEAA).
We are satisfied that the variations sought are necessary to correct typographical errors, delete redundant provisions and ensure
that current entitlements have not inadvertently been omitted.
We will vary the modern award as sought.
