section 188(1) of the Act in relation to the handling of trust
FULL TRIBUNAL
LEGAL PROFESSION TRIBUNAL ORDERS
1. The legal practitioner, Gary Allen Victor Singer, shall pay a fine of
$10,000.00 to the Legal Practice Board. There will be
a stay until 30 June
2006 for the payment of the fine.
2 The legal practitioner, Gary Allen Victor Singer, shall pay to Law Institute
of Victoria Limited the sum of $8,062.50, being its
costs of these proceedings.
There will be a stay until 30 June 2006 for the payment of those costs.
3. Charges 1 and 3 be withdrawn.
