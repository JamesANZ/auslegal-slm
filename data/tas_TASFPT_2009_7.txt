D Graham v Forest Practices Authority [2009] TASFPT 7 (26 October 2009)
File No:2/08 F D7/2009
D Graham Appellant
Forest Practices Authority Respondent
This was the hearing of an appeal against the decision of the Forest
Practices Authority to refuse certification for Forest Practices
Plan BMR0421,
at 1016 Millwood Road, King Island.
The appeal was heard at Hobart
October 2009
G Wilkinson appeared on behalf of
the Forest Practices Authority.
D Graham appeared in
Tribunal decides and directs the Appellant’s Forest Practices Plan can be
certified without constraint as to threatened
species or land clearing
thresholds.
Tribunal notes Mr Graham’s application for costs in this appeal dated
13 October 2009. The Forest Practices Authority has
21 days to make a
written response to this costs application. Mr Graham is granted 7 days to
reply to those submissions before the
Tribunal makes its ruling as to the matter
Dated this 26
day of October
John Pretty
Philip Wright
Raymond Brereton
Deputy Chief Chairman
