Coalition Members’ Additional Comments61
Migration (Public Interest Criterion 4022—Code of Behaviour) Instrument 2025
Coalition Members’
Migration (Public Interest Criterion 4022—Code
of Behaviour) Instrument 2025
1.1 Coalition members note that this legislative instrument will require
certain Subclass 050 (Bridging (General)) visa holders to
sign an enforceable
code of behaviour.
1.2 The code of behaviour contains a list of expectations about how the
applicant will behave at all times while in Australia.
1.3 Coalition members consider this requirement to be an entirely
proportionate limit on rights and in keeping with the expectations
Australian public.
Mr Henry Pike MP
Member for Bowman
Senator Matt O'Sullivan
Senator for Western Australia
Senator Ross Cadell
Senator for New South Wales
