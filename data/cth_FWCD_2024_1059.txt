ADELAIDE, 14 NOVEMBER 2024
Alteration of other rules of organisation.
On 24 October 2024 The Pharmacy Guild of Australia (PGA) lodged with
the Fair Work Commission (the Commission) a notice and declaration
setting out
particulars of alterations to its rules. Further information in support of the
alterations was lodged on 31 October 2024.
The PGA seeks certification of the alterations under
section 159
Fair Work (Registered Organisations) Act 2009
On the information contained in the notice and declaration, I am
satisfied the alterations have been made under the rules of the organisation.
The particulars:
– Admission to Membership; and
Application for Membership Forms 1 – 4.
The alterations to
broaden the means by which new applicants
can apply for membership, allowing them to do so via the use of online,
electronic or paper-based
forms prescribed by the National Council. As a result,
the standard membership application forms previously contained in the rulebook
(and any references thereto) are now no longer required and have been
In my opinion, the alterations comply with and are not contrary to
the Act, the
Fair Work Act 2009
, modern awards and enterprise agreements,
are not otherwise contrary to law and were made under the rules of the
organisation. I
certify accordingly under
subsection 159(1)
of the Act.
