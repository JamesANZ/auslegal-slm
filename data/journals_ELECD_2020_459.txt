GENERAL BIBLIOGRAPHY
In addition to the references that can be found along with the development of
each chapter, there exist many valuable books on international investment law
to which reference can be made. A drastically reduced selection of the available
books that were used to draft the present textbook is presented here (including
the most important references in French):
Audit, M., S. Bollée, P. Callé, Droit du Commerce International et des Inves-
tissements Etrangers, LGDJ (2014).
Bishop, R.D., J. Crawford, M. Reisman, Foreign Investment Disputes: Cases,
Material and Commentary, Kluwer (2005).
Bugenberg, M., J. Griebel, S. Hobe, A. Reinisch (eds), International Invest-
ment Law: A Handbook, Nomos (2015).
Carreau, D., P. Juillard, A. Hamann, R. Bismuth, Droit International Econom-
ique, Dalloz, 6th edn (2017).
Dolzer, R., C. Schreuer, Principles of International Investment Law, Oxford
University Press (2008).
Dolzer, R., M. Stevens, Bilateral Investment Treaties, Nijhoff (1995).
Douglas, Z., The International Law of Investment Claims, Cambridge Univer-
sity Press (2009).
Dugan, C., D. Wallace Jr., N. Rubins, B. Sabahi, Investor-State Arbitration,
Oxford University Press (2008).
Fouret, J., R. Gerbay, G. Alvarez (eds), The ICSID Convention, Regulations
and Rules, A Practical Commentary, Edward Elgar Publishing (2019).
Kessedjian, C. Droit du Commerce International, PUF (2013).
Kinnear, M., G. R. Fischer, J. Mínguez Almeida, L. Fernanda Torres,
M. Uran Bidegain (eds), Building International Investment Law. The First
50 Years of ICSID, Wolters Kluwer (2016).
