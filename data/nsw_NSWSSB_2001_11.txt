Pawluchenko v Owners Corporation SP30734
[2001] NSWSSB 11
(10 December 2001)
STRATA SCHEMES BOARD
APPLICATION NO : SB01/00175
SCHEME : SP30734
ADDRESS OF : "..."
STRATA SCHEME BATEHAVEN NSW
APPLICANTS : N Pawluchenko
RESPONDENT : The Secretary Owners Corporation
On 23 November 2001
the Tribunal made the following findings and orders.
A. The parties have been unable to agree as to the extent of repairs to the
driveway or their respective contribution to the cost
of removal of the tree
referred to in the orders of 30 May 2001. The application has thus been
re-listed in accordance with the
leave granted on 30 May 2001.
B. In this 2 lot strata scheme there have been no formal meetings of the
proprietors since 1995 and no contributions to either administrative
C. Mrs Pawluchenko went ahead and had the willow tree cut down and stump ground
out without discussing or obtaining the approval
of Mr and Mrs Seymour. Mrs
Pawluchenko paid $330.00 for this work.
D. As the sawn tree trunk and branches were not removed from the property Mr and
Mrs Seymour incurred an amount of $132.00 in having
the vegetation
E. The total cost in having the tree cut down and removed was thus $462.00.
This amount was greater than the quotation which had
been obtained by Mr and Mrs
Seymour to do the entire job.
F. Nevertheless, and in the absence of any maintenance fund, the owners should
contribute equally to the actual cost of this work.
Each party is thus required
to contribute $231.00. As Mr and Mrs Seymour have spent $132.00 an additional
$99.00 is to be paid
by them. Mr & Mrs Seymour have paid $44.00 to Mrs
Pawluchenko leaving a balance of $55.00.
G. The parties agree that no repairs are presently necessary to the driveway now
that the tree has been removed.
1. The other parties to the application, Mr and Mrs Seymour, are to pay the
applicant, Mrs Pawluchenko, the sum of $55.00 within
2. The order of 30 May 2001 insofar as it requires the respondent owners
corporation to repair the driveway is set aside. The orders
made on that day
are otherwise confirmed.
3. No order as to costs.
