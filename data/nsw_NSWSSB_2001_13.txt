Kalaf Investments Pty Ltd v Hutchins & ors
(6 September 2001)
STRATA SCHEMES BOARD OF NEW SOUTH
APPLICATION NO : SB01/00258
STRATA SCHEME : SP
ADDRESS OF : "...", Cooranbong NSW 2265
APPLICANTS : Kalaf Investments Pty Ltd
RESPONDENT : BB. Hutchins, ME.Hutchins, Dentraz Pty
Ltd, S.J Nurrish, K.J.
Wood, RD.Dixon,
APPEARANCES : Mr.M Neate for the applicant
Issues: Section 182
Strata Schemes Management Act
Strata Schemes (Freehold Development) Act 1973
words : Initial period restrictions.
Pursuant to
section 182
Strata Schemes Management Act 1996
an order is
made authorising the following acts during the initial period:
1. The registration of a strata plan of subdivision that complies with
Strata Schemes (Freehold Development) Act 1973
and that relates to the
subdivision of lots comprising strata scheme 57931. A copy of the proposed plan
is annexed hereto and marked
"Annexure A".
REASONS FOR DECISION
The application was lodged in the Registry of the Strata Schemes Board (the
Board) on 2 May 2001. The orders sought are that pursuant
section 182
Strata Schemes Management Act 1996
the Board waive the restriction imposed
Strata Schemes (Freehold Development) Act 1973
The original owner proposes to undertake a boundary re-alignment by sub-division
of lots 2 and 3 in SP 57931. The Board was satisfied
the initial period has not
yet expired. The purpose of the strata plan of sub-division is a boundary
re-alignment of lots 2 and
3. The total area of the building comprising the
scheme remains unchanged. There is no effect upon the external boundaries.
There were no submissions opposing the application.
