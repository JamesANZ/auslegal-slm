WOTTON, Jeremy Dane - Inquest Number 06/2022 (1038/2018), Responses [2022] SACorCResp 6 (13 July 2022)
Wotton, Jeremy Dane (Coroner Kereru)
Recommendation 1
- That consideration is given to regular training for
Emergency Department staff in the symptoms of posterior circulation stroke.
Response from SA Health
supported by LHNs. It will be followed up through clinical education, and change
management (the Towards Zero approach).
Recommendation 2
That consideration is given to developing a clear pathway for Emergency
Department clinicians to follow when symptoms of vertigo
or dizziness are both
episodic and continuous.
Response from SA Health
supported by LHNs. It will be followed up through clinical education, and change
management (the Towards Zero approach).
Recommendation 3
That consideration is given to engaging the on-call Stroke Unit when the HINTS
examination is considered to be indicated in the
Emergency Department.
Response from SA Health
supported by LHNs. It will be followed up through clinical education, and change
management (the Towards Zero approach).
