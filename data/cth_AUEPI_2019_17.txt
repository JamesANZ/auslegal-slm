Fuel Quality Standards (Ethanol E85) Information Standard 2019 [2019] AUEPI 17 (27 September 2019)
Fuel Quality Standards (Ethanol E85) Information Standard 2019
I, Angus Taylor, Minister for Energy and Emissions Reduction, determine the
following information standard.
Dated 18 September 2019
Angus Taylor
Minister for Energy and Emissions Reduction
Fuel Quality Information Standard (Ethanol E85)
Determination 2012
This instrument is the
Fuel Quality Standards (Ethanol E85) Information
Standard 2019
Commencement
This instrument commences on 1 October 2019.
This instrument is made under
section 22A
Fuel Quality
Standards Act 2000
Each instrument that is specified in a Schedule to this instrument is
amended or repealed as set out in the applicable items in
the Schedule
concerned, and any other item in a Schedule to this instrument has effect
according to its terms.
Definitions
In this instrument:
Fuel Quality Standards Act 2000
means a fuel blend consisting of:
(a) 70% to 85% ethanol; and
(b) petrol that meets the requirements of the
Fuel Quality Standards
(Petrol) Determination 2019
service station
means premises at which E85 is sold by retail
from a fuel pump.
Supply of E85 from service
(1) This section specifies:
(a) the information about E85 that should be provided with the supply of E85
from a service station; and
(b) the way in which that information is to be provided.
(2) One of the following is to be clearly displayed on any fuel pump at the
service station dispensing E85:
(a) the words:
(i) “Contains 70–85% ethanol”; and
(ii) “Not petrol or diesel”;
(b) the words:
(i) “Contains x% ethanol”, where x is a number equal to or more
than 70 but not more than 85 and is no less than the
percentage of ethanol in
the ethanol blend; and
(ii) “Not petrol or diesel”.
(3) For the purposes of subparagraph (2)(b)(i), if a percentage is not
a whole number, it may be rounded to the nearest whole number,
with a number
ending with 0.5 being rounded up.
Other retail supply of E85
(1) This section specifies:
(a) the information about E85 that should be provided in relation to the
supply of E85, by retail sale, other than from a service
station; and
(b) the way in which that information is to be provided.
(2) Either of the following:
(a) before the E85 is supplied to a buyer, the buyer is to be given a
document that prominently includes the words specified in paragraph
(b) if the buyer is present when the E85 is supplied, the words specified in
paragraph 6(2)(a) or (b) are to appear on each container
of E85 so that the
words can be easily read by the buyer.
Supply of E85 to retailers
(1) This section specifies:
(a) the information about E85 that should be provided with the supply of E85
to a retailer; and
(b) the way in which that information is to be provided.
(2) At the time of the supply, the retailer is to be given a document that
prominently displays:
(a) a statement that the E85 contains:
(i) 70% to 85% ethanol; or
(ii) x% ethanol, where x is a number equal to or more than 70 but not more
than 85 and is no less than the percentage of ethanol
in the ethanol blend;
(b) a statement that the E85 is the subject of this instrument.
