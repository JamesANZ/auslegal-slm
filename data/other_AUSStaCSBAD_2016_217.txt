National Health Amendment (Pharmaceutical Benefits) Bill 2016
National Health Amendment (Pharmaceutical Benefits)
This bill seeks to amend the
National Health Act 1953
• allow computer programs to be used for certain administrative
decisions and actions;
• reduce administrative requirements for pharmacists needing to
operate from alternative premises following disasters or exceptional
circumstances; and
• amend a definition to ensure that PBS entitlements work as intended
for concessional beneficiaries and their dependants on
the day a person
House of Representatives on 24 November 2016
The committee has no comment on this bill.
