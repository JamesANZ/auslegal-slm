Greenhouse and Energy Minimum Standards (Registration Fees) Instrument (No. 2) 2019 [2019] AUEPI 8 (15 August 2019)
Greenhouse and Energy Minimum Standards
(Registration Fees) Instrument (No. 2) 2019
I, Michelle Croker, GEMS Regulator, acting in accordance with my powers under
subsection 8
Greenhouse and Energy Minimum Standards
(Registration Fees) Act 2012
, make this Instrument for the purpose of
specifying the fees payable in relation to applications under
Greenhouse and Energy Minimum Standards Act 2012
Michelle Croker
GEMS Regulator
Dated 9 August 2019
Preliminary
Name of Instrument
This Instrument is
Greenhouse and Energy Minimum Standards (Registration Fees) Instrument
(No. 2) 2019.
Commencement
This Instrument commences
on the day after it is registered.
This Instrument revokes the
Greenhouse and Energy
Minimum Standards (Registration Fees) Instrument 2019
The object of this Instrument
is to specify, for the purposes of
subsection 8
and Energy Minimum Standards (Registration Fees) Act 2012
the fees payable
in relation to applications under
Greenhouse and Energy
Minimum Standards Act 2012
to register models of GEMS products.
Interpretation
(1) Unless the contrary
intention appears words and expressions used in this
Instrument have the same meaning as given in the
Greenhouse and Energy Minimum Standards Act 2012
and Energy Minimum Standards (Registration Fees) Act 2012
(2) The descriptions in column 4 of Schedule 1 are a generic description of
the products covered by the GEMS Determinations listed
in column 3 of Schedule
1. They are provided for ease of reference only. Whether a model is covered by a
GEMS Determination is determined
by the relevant GEMS determination listed in
column 3 of Schedule 1.
Column 2 of Schedule 1 sets out the
registration fees specified for registration applications in relation to models
of GEMS products
covered by the GEMS determinations listed in column 3 of
Schedule 1.
