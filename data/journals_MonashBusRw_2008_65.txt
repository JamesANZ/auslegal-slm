UPCOMING RESEARCH
The benefits of investing in communities: Identifying the potential for building stronger and more inclusive communities
Researchers: Lionel Frost (Economics), Andrea Kirk-Brown (Management), Abdel Halabi (Accounting & Finance), PieterVan Dijk (Management).
This project seeks to develop new measures of the effectiveness of strategies used by sporting bodies to strengthen connections with
the communities within which they work. Australian Football clubs and netball and cricket organisations have agreed to act as research
partners for an ARC Linkage application.
