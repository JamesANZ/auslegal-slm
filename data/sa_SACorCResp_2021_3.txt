clinical resources in respect of mental health services in all rural regions

Continuity of

within the health system and
plays an important role in assisting in therapeutic
engagement with the individual in care. It is also dependent on good systems
procedures and having the appropriate resources to implement those systems and
procedures. A review of the services
will assist in determining where gaps may
exist and what changes may need to be implemented to improve continuity of care
in regional services.
