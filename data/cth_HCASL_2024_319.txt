ATTORNEY-GENERAL FOR THE STATE OF

[2024] HCASL 319
is an application for an order under
Judiciary Act 1903
(Cth) removing the whole of a cause now pending in the Supreme Court of
Victoria, being proceeding number S ECI 2023 02355. The applicant
seeks an order under
Judiciary Act
that the matter be
remitted to the Supreme Court of Victoria. The purpose of the orders sought is
to avoid a dispute as to the jurisdiction
of the Supreme Court of Victoria to
hear and determine the proceeding. The proceeding concerns a statutory charge
over land formerly
used as a retirement village under the
Retirement Villages
(Vic) and includes questions arising under the
Constitution
or involving its interpretation. In the circumstances, it is appropriate to make
the orders sought.
Judiciary Act
, the whole of the cause now pending
in the Supreme Court of Victoria, which is proceeding number S ECI 2023
02355, be removed into
this Court.
Judiciary Act
, the matter be remitted to the
Supreme Court of Victoria.
be no order as to costs.
Beech-Jones J
December 2024
