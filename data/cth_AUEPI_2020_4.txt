Greenhouse and Energy Minimum Standards (Refrigerated Cabinets Revocation)
Instrument 2020
I, Angus Taylor, Minister for Energy and Emissions Reduction, make the
following instrument.
Dated 5 August 2020
Angus Taylor
Minister for Energy and Emissions Reduction
Part 1—Preliminary
This instrument is the
Greenhouse and Energy
Minimum Standards (Refrigerated Cabinets Revocation) Instrument 2020
2 Commencement
This instrument commences and comes into force on the day that it is made.
3 Authority
This instrument is made under
Greenhouse and Energy
Minimum Standards Act 2012
Definitions
In this instrument:
commencement day
means the day on which this instrument comes
into force.
refrigerated cabinets determination
Greenhouse and Energy Minimum Standards (Refrigerated Cabinets)
Determination 2019
—Revocation
5 Revocation
(1) This instrument revokes the refrigerated cabinets determination.
(2) The refrigerated cabinets determination ceases to be in force on the
commencement day.
(3) If the refrigerated cabinets determination is not yet in force on the
commencement day, subsection (2) has the effect that the
refrigerated cabinets
determination never comes into force.
Note: The form of this section reflects the requirements of
Act, which deal with how a GEMS determination may be revoked without being
Subsection 35(4)
of the Act provides that, when a GEMS
determination is revoked without being replaced, the revoked determination
ceases to be in
force at the time specified in the instrument of revocation.
