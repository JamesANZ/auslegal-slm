TROTTA, Giovanni - Inquest Number 54/2020 (1242/2018), Responses [2023] SACorCResp 8 (10 November 2023)
Trotta, Giovanni (Coroner White)
Recommendation
- The Standard Operating
Procedure for DCS regarding Code Black calls in correctional institutions be
reviewed to consider including
terms of reference for direction of orders and
requests by SAPHS medical staff to COs during a Code Black emergency.
Response from Department for Corrections
The purpose of
Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) 020A Code Black - Medical Emergency is to
describe the procedure for DCS staff to
follow in the event of a medical
In accordance
with sections 3.2.2 and 3.8.9 of the SOP, it is the responsibility of DCS staff
to contact the necessary emergency services,
such as the SA Ambulance Service
and the South Australia Police.
Following a
review by DCS into the death of Mr Trotta, communication was distributed to
relevant staff by way of a Deputy Chief Executive
Notification (DCEN). The DCEN
reminded staff of the requirement, in accordance with the SOP, that the
Supervisor/Officer in Charge
is to direct the Control Room Officer to contact
the necessary emergency services and agencies where it is considered that
assistance is required.
