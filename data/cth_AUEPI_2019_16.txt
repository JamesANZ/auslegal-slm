Fuel Quality Standards (Ethanol) Information Standard 2019 [2019] AUEPI 16 (27 September 2019)
Fuel Quality Standards (Ethanol) Information Standard 2019
I, Angus Taylor, Minister for Energy and Emissions Reduction, determine the
following information standard.
Dated 18 September 2019
Angus Taylor
Minister for Energy and Emissions Reduction
Fuel Quality Information Standard (Ethanol)
Determination 2003
This instrument is the
Fuel Quality Standards (Ethanol) Information
Standard 2019
Commencement
This instrument commences on 1 October 2019.
This instrument is made under
section 22A
Fuel Quality
Standards Act 2000
Each instrument that is specified in a Schedule to this instrument is
amended or repealed as set out in the applicable items in
the Schedule
concerned, and any other item in a Schedule to this instrument has effect
according to its terms.
Definitions
In this instrument:
ethanol blend
means petrol that, as tested in accordance with
Fuel Quality Standards (Petrol) Determination 2019
more than 1% ethanol.
service station
means premises at which petrol is sold by
retail from a fuel pump.
Supply of ethanol blend from service
(1) This section specifies:
(a) the information about ethanol blend that should be provided with the
supply of ethanol blend from a service station; and
(b) the way in which that information is to be provided.
(2) One of the following is to be clearly displayed on any fuel pump at the
service station dispensing ethanol blend:
(a) the words “Contains up to x% ethanol”, where
is no less than the percentage of ethanol in the ethanol blend;
(b) the words “Contains y% ethanol”, where
is the percentage of ethanol in the ethanol blend.
(3) For the purposes of paragraph (2)(b), if the percentage is not a
whole number, it may be rounded to the nearest whole number,
with a number
ending with 0.5 being rounded up.
Other retail supply of ethanol
(1) This section specifies:
(a) the information about ethanol blend that should be provided in relation
to the supply of ethanol blend, by retail sale, other
than from a service
station; and
(b) the way in which that information is to be provided.
(2) Either of the following:
(a) before the ethanol blend is supplied to a buyer, the buyer is to be
given a document that prominently includes the words specified
paragraph 6(2)(a) or (b);
(b) if the buyer is present when the ethanol blend is supplied, the words
specified in paragraph 6(2)(a) or (b) are to appear on
each container of
ethanol blend so that the words can be easily read by the buyer.
Supply of ethanol blend to
(1) This section specifies:
(a) the information about ethanol blend that should be provided with the
supply of ethanol blend to a retailer; and
(b) the way in which that information is to be provided.
(2) At the time of the supply, the retailer is to be given a document that
prominently displays:
(a) a statement that the ethanol blend may contain up to and including 10%
ethanol; and
(b) a statement that the ethanol blend is the subject of this
instrument.
Fuel Quality Information Standard (Ethanol)
Determination 2003
1 The whole of the instrument
Repeal the instrument.
