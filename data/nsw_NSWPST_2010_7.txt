Introduction
HCCC made Complaint dated 17 December 2008 regarding the conduct of the
Respondent. The Complaint was referred to the Tribunal
on 9 July 2009. A
Directions Hearing was held on 10 September 2009 and the dates for an Inquiry
were set for 9 to 12 March 2010 inclusive.
Further Directions Hearing was held on 12 February 2010 at the request of the
Respondent. The Respondent provided medical reports
indicating he was ill. A
request was made for the hearing to be postponed and the hearing dates in August
Death Certificate was provided to the Tribunal on 20 May 2010 recording that the
Respondent died on 21 March 2010.
accordance with Clause 12, Schedule 5 of the
Psychologists Act 2001
the Tribunal
will not conduct an Inquiry into the Complaint dated 17 December 2008.
