23-25 February 2009
Maritime Economics Seminar
Bonhill House, London
Speakers from maritime commerce and academia will analyse the current
situation in various sectors of industry. Presentations consist
of the global
trade and effects of the emerging markets; the costs of operating ships and the
effect of various outside influences;
the economics of the shipping markets with
emphasis on the different sectors; capital for shipping and the various risk
tools available.
Further information: Laura Tavergnier, phone +44 (0) 20 7017 4657, email
laura.tavergnier@informa.com
www.lloydsmaritimeacademy.com/lw1006
