determination

5 Exemption
The models specified in clause 1 of the Schedule (the
) are exempt from the requirements of the Determination as
described at clause 2 of the Schedule with respect to the supplies of the
models specified at clause 3 of the Schedule.
6 Conditions of exemption
There are no conditions specified for the purposes of
subsection 37(2)
Subsection 37(2)
of the Act provides that the exemption may specify
conditions to which the exemption is subject.
Subsection 37(3)
provides that a
person must comply with a condition of an exemption to the extent that it
applies to the person.
This instrument is repealed on 1 January 2020.
