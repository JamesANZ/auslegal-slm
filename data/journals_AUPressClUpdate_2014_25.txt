“Due to serious misrepresentations today in at least a couple of media
outlets, it should be noted that my statement on Tuesday

general media coverage of the incident.
Indeed it said ‘Much of the
coverage has been excellent and has not hesitated to tell painful truths when
necessary’.
While expressing concern about some errors and exaggerations, it did not
criticise any particular material or publication and was
not based on any
particular complaint to the Council. This reflects the fact that the roles of
the Council and its Chair are not
confined to expressing views on particular
complaints.
