R v Ryan [1890] NSWSupC 2 (12 April 1890)
[Aboriginal defendant, assault, wounding police officer, arrest without warrant, escape, right of]
Central Criminal Court
Sydney Morning Herald
, 12 April 1890
At the Central Criminal Court Tommy Ryan, the aboriginal, was sentenced to four years' penal servitude for wounding a mounted
constable Thomas Atkins, for a criminal assault.
This very brief report led to an appeal which was reported as
(1890) 11 LR (NSW) 171
6 WN (NSW) 162.
The conviction was affirmed. This appeal was also reported in the
Sydney Morning Herald
10 May 1890
16 June 1890
