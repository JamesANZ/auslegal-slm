Singleton v Kelly [1887] NSWSupC 1 (12 December 1887)
[breach of promise of marriage]
Singleton v. Kelly
Supreme Court of New South Wales
Stephen J., 12 December 1887
Sydney Mornnig Herald
, 13 December 1887, p. 3
Darlinghurst Court. - (Before his Honor Mr. Justice Stephen and a jury of four.)
Mr. Hanbury Davis, instructed by Mr. Gillespie, appeared for the plaintiff; and Mr. Coffey, instructed by Messrs. Bradley and Son,
for the defendant. This was an action brought by Emma Louisa Singleton against Augustin James Kelly, to recover damages for breach
of promise of marriage.
Mr. Davis opened the case, and counsel having then consulted together, the action was settled, the defendant consenting to a verdict
for the plaintiff for ï¿½125, with taxed costs.
The jury, under his Honor's direction, returned a verdict for that amount.
