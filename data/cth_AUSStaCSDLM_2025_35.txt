Undertakings
4.1 This Chapter identifies the new undertakings
that have been made in relation to instruments considered in this report and
that the committee is aware have been implemented as at 22 August 2025.
4.2 The committee expects undertakings to be implemented in a timely manner
and to be kept informed on their progress until implemented.
This Chapter lists
undertakings that are outstanding for more than 12 months and those outstanding
for more than 90 days as at 22
August 2025.
