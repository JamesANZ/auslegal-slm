Riley, Judith and Donald (2023 TASCD 524), Responses [2023] TasCorCResp 4 (26 June 2023)
Response received from Department of State Growth, State Roads Division 2 October 2023
I note the record of investigation and findings, handed down on 26 June 2023, with respect to the deaths of Judith Anne Riley and
Donald Roy Riley. A copy was provided to the Department by your office on 15 September 2023.
The record of investigation noted a report from the Department of State Growth that indicated that slippery when wet signs were to
be provided as an interim measure on this section of the West Tamar Highway, with resealing works to occur at a later date.
I can confirm that the Department completed resealing works at this location in February 2022 as part of its annual maintenance program,
